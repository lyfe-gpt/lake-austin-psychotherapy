Questions

Good to know

How do I know which service is right for me?

That’s exactly what your free 1-hour consultation is for. We’ll talk through what brings you in and recommend the best fit. Sometimes it’s one modality, sometimes a combination.

Do you offer telehealth for all services?

EMDR and grief therapy are available via secure video statewide. Ketamine-assisted sessions take place in person; we provide private transportation from Austin, Dallas, Houston and San Antonio.

What does care cost? Is there a sliding scale?

Rates are discussed in your free consultation, and a sliding scale is available. We’ll also explain the Good Faith Estimate and No Surprises Act so there are no surprises.

How quickly can I start?

We have no waitlist. Most clients begin within days of their consultation. Reach out and we’ll find a time that works for you.
