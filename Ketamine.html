Questions

Good to know

Is ketamine therapy safe?

Ketamine has been used safely in medicine since the 1960s. In our program every session is physician-administered and monitored, with care tailored to your history.

What does a session feel like?

Most people experience a gentle, dreamlike shift in awareness. A clinician is present throughout, and we process the experience together in follow-up therapy.

How much does it cost? Is there a sliding scale?

Rates are discussed in your free consultation, and a sliding scale is available. We'll also explain the Good Faith Estimate and No Surprises Act.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes. Private luxury transportation is available for our ketamine clients from Austin, Dallas, Houston and San Antonio.
