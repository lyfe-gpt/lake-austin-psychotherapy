Common questions

Good to know before you reach out

What types of therapy does Courtney Kurtz offer?

I offer EMDR therapy, Internal Family Systems (IFS), ketamine-assisted psychotherapy, Prolonged Grief Disorder therapy, talk therapy, and CBT. All services address anxiety, depression, trauma, PTSD, and grief. EMDR and grief therapy are available via telehealth throughout Texas; ketamine sessions are in-person in Austin.

How quickly can I start therapy?

There is no waitlist. Most clients begin within days of their free 1-hour consultation. I respond to all inquiries seven days a week by call, email, or text.

Do you offer therapy via telehealth?

Yes. EMDR and grief therapy are available via secure, HIPAA-compliant video anywhere in Texas. You need to be physically located in Texas during sessions, as I am licensed in Texas. Ketamine sessions are in-person in Austin.

What does the free 1-hour consultation include?

A warm, judgment-free 1-hour Zoom conversation at no cost. You’ll discuss what brings you in, hear a recommendation on which modalities fit your history and goals, and get a clear picture of rates. No commitment required.

Do you accept insurance?

My practice is out-of-network with insurance providers. Many clients submit for out-of-network reimbursement using a superbill I provide. PPO plans often cover a significant portion of costs. Rates are discussed openly in your free consultation.
