Common questions

Good to know before you reach out

What types of therapy does Lake Austin Psychotherapy offer?

We offer EMDR therapy, physician-administered ketamine-assisted psychotherapy, Prolonged Grief Disorder therapy using Columbia University’s protocol, talk therapy, and CBT. All services treat anxiety, depression, trauma, PTSD, and grief. EMDR and grief therapy are available via telehealth throughout Texas; ketamine sessions are in-person with private transportation from Austin, Dallas, Houston, and San Antonio.

How quickly can I start therapy?

There is no waitlist. Most clients begin within days of their free 1-hour consultation. We respond to all inquiries seven days a week by call, email, or text.

Do you offer therapy via telehealth?

Yes. EMDR and grief therapy are available via secure, HIPAA-compliant video anywhere in Texas. You need to be physically located in Texas during sessions, as our clinicians are licensed in Texas. Ketamine infusions are in-person; private transportation is provided from Austin, Dallas, Houston, and San Antonio.

What does the free 1-hour consultation include?

A warm, judgment-free 1-hour Zoom conversation at no cost. You’ll discuss what brings you in, hear a recommendation on which modalities fit your history and goals, and get a clear picture of rates and the sliding scale. No commitment required.

Do you accept insurance?

Lake Austin Psychotherapy is out-of-network with insurance providers. Many clients submit for out-of-network reimbursement using a superbill we provide. PPO plans often cover a significant portion of costs. A sliding scale fee is available and discussed openly in your free consultation.
