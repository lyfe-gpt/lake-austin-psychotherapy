Before you reach out

Quick answers

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, completely. The free 1-hour consultation is a full Zoom conversation with a clinician, at no cost and no obligation. You’ll discuss what brings you in, get a modality recommendation, and hear about rates. We ask nothing of you except honesty about what you’re dealing with.

How quickly will I hear back?

We respond to all inquiries within one business day, and usually the same day. You can also call or text directly: 512·666·1184. We respond seven days a week.

Is there a waitlist?

Scheduling is flexible. I will reach out promptly after you submit the form to find a time that works. Most clients start within a few weeks of their first inquiry.

What happens after I submit the form?

A clinician reviews your message and reaches out within one business day to schedule your free 1-hour consultation via Zoom. If it’s urgent, call 512·666·1184 directly. There is no obligation after the consultation and no pressure to proceed.
