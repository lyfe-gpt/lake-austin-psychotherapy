Questions

Good to know

Is telehealth therapy as effective as in-person?

Yes. Multiple peer-reviewed meta-analyses confirm that telehealth therapy achieves outcomes equivalent to in-person care for anxiety, depression, PTSD, and grief. EMDR has been validated for remote delivery specifically, with the same efficacy rates as office sessions.

Do I need to be physically in Texas?

Yes. Our clinicians are licensed in Texas, so you need to be physically located in Texas during sessions. If you travel frequently, as long as you’re in-state when we meet, we can continue care.

What do I need to get started?

Any device with a camera and microphone, or a smartphone, works fine. A stable internet connection and a private, quiet space to talk. We’ll send a secure link before your session; no apps or accounts required.

Is my session completely private?

Yes. We use HIPAA-compliant, end-to-end encrypted video that meets all federal privacy standards. Sessions are never recorded without explicit consent.

How quickly can I start?

We have flexible scheduling. Most clients begin when ready of their free consultation. Reach out and we’ll find a time that works around your schedule.
